Our Story

A landmark born
in Eastern Nepal

Background

The first international
hotel in Sunsari

Ramada by Wyndham Itahari is a landmark hospitality achievement — the first international hotel chain to establish a presence in Eastern Nepal, beyond Kathmandu. Located at Pashupati Marg, Itahari-4, Sunsari District, the property opened its doors under the AB Holding group.

The hotel is managed under the Wyndham Hotels & Resorts franchise — a global hospitality company operating in 95+ countries with 9,100+ hotels under 24 distinct brands. Ramada is one of Wyndham's most storied upper-midscale brands.

Strategically located in Itahari — a rapidly growing commercial and transit hub connecting Kathmandu with the Terai and the Indian border — the hotel serves business travellers, wedding parties, corporate events, and leisure guests from across the Koshi Province.

With 81 rooms, multiple dining outlets, an outdoor pool, health club, spa, and grand banquet halls with capacity for 300 guests, Ramada by Wyndham Itahari represents a transformative moment for hospitality in Eastern Nepal.
